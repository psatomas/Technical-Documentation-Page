Owner's Manual

Important Safety Instructions
  • Do not use this device for other than its intended purpose.
  • Exercise caution and use reasonable supervision when device is used near children. Never allow children to operate or play with unit.
  • Intended for indoor household use only.
  • Locate the unit on a steady surface away from direct flames and other sources of heat (stove, heater, radiator, etc).
  • Do not immerse in water or other liquids. Avoid locating the unit in moist areas.
  • For safety and best results, do not operate unit in an ambient temperature outside the range of 32°-95°F.
  • Do not use the unit as a handle to lift or move the bottle. This may cause the bottle to fall from the appliance.
  • Do not operate if unit, cord or plug is damaged. Return to the manufacturer for examination or repair.
  • When charging, always plug DC cord into unit first, then plug AC cord into electrical outlet. To disconnect, remove the AC cord first then the DC power cord. Always unplug the unit before taking off parts or attachments.

WARNING: Electric Wine Opener corkscrew is sharp. Handle with care. Hold the opener by its outer housing only. Keep hands and objects away from mechanism during operation to prevent injury or damage.

Charging Your Bottle Opener

  • Plug DC connector into unit, then plug AC end into electrical outlet.
  • Place Bottle Opener into charging base properly to ensure charge.
  • Charging base will start working automatically
  • When the charging process is complete, the blue indicator light will glow

Opening A Sealed Bottle
  • Remove any foil from the bottle by using the magnetized foil cutter included. While holding the lower half of the bottle firmly, affix the foil cutter to the top of the bottle and twist to remove foil.
  • Ensure that the bottle remains upright, and then place the bottle opener firmly against the top of the bottle. Holding the bottle opener at an angle to the bottle may result in improper removal of cork.
  • Press the lower button to engage the opener. The corkscrew spiral will now turn counterclockwise and enter the cork, gradually removing it from the bottle. When the cork has been fully removed, the Electric Wine Opener will stop.
  • Lift the bottle opener away from the bottle. Press the upper button and the corkscrew spiral will turn clockwise and release the cork.
  • During periods of inactivity, return the opener to its charging base.

Care And Maintenance
  • Always store your Electric Wine Opener in a cool, dry place.
  • Always disconnect before cleaning. To clean, wipe housing with a soft and dry cloth. Do not use liquid or abrasive cleaning agents.

Important Disposal Instructions

As a responsible company, we care about the environment. As such, we urge you to follow the correct disposal procedures for your product, batteries, and packaging materials. This will help conserve natural resources and ensure that it is recycled in a manner that protects health and the environment. You must dispose of this product and its packaging according to local laws and regulations. Because this product contains electronic components and sometimes batteries, the product and its accessories must be disposed of separately from household waste when the product reaches its end of life cycle. The product and batteries should be taken to a proper recycling/disposal facility.
